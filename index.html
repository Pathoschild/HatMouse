Mod jam prizes

This page shows the prizes you can win in the Stardew Valley Discord's mod jam event.

You can choose any combination of prizes up to $30 (first place), $20 (second place), and $10 (third place). For team projects, you can add $5 per extra member (up to $20 extra).

The title column is the name of the game key. All other info (including the link to the Steam page) was filled in based on the name, so there may be discrepencies.
